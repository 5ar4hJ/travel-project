Why Lauterbrunnen?

Lauterbrunnen, an enchanting valley with cascading waterfalls

Lauterbrunnen rests at an elevation of 795 meters in a stunning, deep valley cradling picturesque villages like Gimmelwald, Isenfluh, Lauterbrunnen, Mürren, Stechelberg, and Wengen. Towering cliffs and peaks surround the area, with 72 waterfalls tumbling down into the valley. Among the most renowned are Staubbach Falls and Trümmelbach Falls

In Lauterbrunnen, a realm of enchantment unfolds amidst snowy peaks, ancient forests, and crystalline waters. Here, waterfalls cascade like silken threads from dizzying heights, veiling the landscape in mist and magic. The air is crisp, carrying the scent of pine and adventure. Exploring Lauterbrunnen means immersing yourself in a world where wooden chalets perch amidst emerald meadows, each one a picture-perfect tableau against the backdrop of towering cliffs. Wander along cobblestone streets lined with charming shops offering treasures waiting to be discovered. A journey towards Staubbach Falls beckons, where the sheer force of nature's spectacle mesmerizes. Venture up the trail behind the falls to unveil panoramic views of the Lauterbrunnen Valley, a vista that whispers tales of legends and dreams

Popular Trails in Lauterbrunnen

Staubbachfälle

Suitable for all skill levels, free, preferrably for age 10+ years. Short walk from train station. Dress appropriately,gets wet. Can go behind falls.

Address

3825 Lauterbrunnen, Switzerland

What I like about it

Captivating panoramic vistas where the falls gracefully complement the exquisite landscape, weaving a harmony that whispers of untamed beauty beneath their thundering cascade

Along the Weisse Lütschine River

Suitable for all skill levels, perfect for evening stroll, running. Immerse yourself in breathtaking scenery.

Address

3824 Lauterbrunnen, Switzerland

Fun fact about it

Its other half is Schwarze (Black) Lütschine. Villagers on the Weisse Lütschine river called those on the Schwarze Lütschine 'so dirty it turned the river black,' while those on the Schwarze Lütschine claimed the others 'never washed, so their tributary stayed perfectly white'

Trümmelbach Falls

Suitable for all skill levels, easy walk, affordable. Children under 4 not permitted. Has a charming outdoor café offering delightful pastries.

Address

3824 Lauterbrunnen, Switzerland

What I like about it

Discover ten glacier-fed waterfalls tucked inside a mountain. Its volume, force, and grandeur make it a spectacle worth seeing. Uniquely accessible via lifts, galleries, tunnels, paths, and platforms—a world-first marvel of underground accessibility
